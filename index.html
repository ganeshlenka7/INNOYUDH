About Inno Yudh

Inno Yudh 2K25 is a 24-hour National Level Hackathon organized by the Department of ECE, NSRIT. It unites creative minds to solve real-world challenges using technology and innovation.
Build, collaborate, and make an impact through your ideas!

Whether you're a coding wizard, a design guru, or a business strategist, Inno Yudh 2K25 welcomes all innovators to showcase their skills and compete for exciting prizes.
